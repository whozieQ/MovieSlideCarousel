Movies I Like

(even though they had low scores on Rotten Tomatoes)

John Carter

I loved the combination of space epic with humor. The world felt fresh and the characters more robust than sci-fi 'fluff' usually is.

Jupiter Ascending

The campy characters took themselves seriously and I found that engaging and fun, not to mention it's visually stunning. Plus -- those gravity skate boots?! Wowza!

This Means War

Chris Pine and Tom Hardy were sweet, even if they wouldn't really make great spies. This is a great combo of action, romance and silly fun!
